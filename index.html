About GenAI Sui

GenAI Sui Wallet is an innovative Web3 wallet built on the high-performance Sui blockchain. It combines the power of artificial intelligence with the flexibility of decentralized finance, enabling users to manage crypto assets and NFTs through natural language prompts.

Vision

To make decentralized finance and blockchain interactions intuitive and universally accessible through artificial intelligence.

Mission

  • Provide seamless AI-powered crypto transactions.
  • Empower users to control their assets through voice or text.
  • Drive Web3 adoption through intuitive design and zero-friction UX.

Roadmap

Q2 2025 - Launch

Initial wallet launch, AI prompt integration, NFT support, and beta release on Android.

Q3 2025 - Expansion

Launch on iOS, integration with major dApps, fiat onramp partnerships.

Q4 2025 - Ecosystem Growth

Release of GenAI SDK, DAO voting system, cross-chain features, and developer grants.

Q1 2026 - Global Adoption

Global marketing push, multi-language support, AI-driven investment tools.
